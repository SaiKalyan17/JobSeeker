Marketing Manager

Bhubaneswar,India Full Time Rs40,000-Rs1,00,000

Job description

The Marketing Manager will be responsible for developing and implementing marketing strategies to promote our company's products and services. The successful candidate will lead a team of marketing professionals and work closely with cross-functional teams to develop and execute campaigns that will drive brand awareness, engagement, and revenue growth.

Responsibility

  • Develop and execute comprehensive marketing plans and strategies to achieve company goals and objectives.
  • Lead the development and implementation of marketing campaigns across multiple channels, including social media, email marketing, advertising, events, and PR.
  • Create and manage the marketing budget, ensuring maximum return on investment.
  • Manage and mentor a team of marketing professionals to deliver high-quality campaigns and content that align with the company's vision and goals.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including sales, product development, and customer service, to ensure marketing initiatives are integrated with business strategies and objectives.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of experience in marketing, with at least 3 years of experience in a management role.
  • Strong leadership skills with the ability to inspire and motivate a team to deliver outstanding results.
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, with the ability to present and communicate effectively with all levels of an organization.
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret data and make informed decisions.
